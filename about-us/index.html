About Hamilton Care Dental Centre

Hamilton Care Dental Centre is a family-focused dental clinic on Hamilton Mountain. Since 2012, we've cared for thousands of patients with thorough, gentle dentistry and a calm, friendly atmosphere. Our team includes three dentists, registered dental hygienists, assistants, and a treatment-coordination and front-office team that handles insurance, CDCP, and Beautifi financing so cost is never a barrier to care.

We believe in conservative dentistry first. That means starting with the simplest option that delivers a great long-term result, explaining everything clearly, and giving you a written estimate before any major treatment.

Owner / Lead Dentist

Dr. Fadi Dawood

Dr. Fadi Dawood opened Hamilton Care Dental Centre in 2012 with a single goal: build the kind of family dental practice where every patient is known by name and every treatment plan is explained fully. 14+ years and 100+ five-star reviews later, that's still the standard he holds the team to.

Dr. Dawood treats patients of all ages and focuses on dental implants, complex restorative cases, and family dentistry. Patients describe him most often as kind, patient, and thorough.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the practice, our team, and visiting us.

Who is the owner of Hamilton Care Dental Centre?

Dr. Fadi Dawood is the owner and founding dentist. He opened the practice on Upper Ottawa Street in 2012 and is supported by associate dentists Dr. Bassam Petros and Dr. Peter Markho, plus a team of registered dental hygienists, a denturist, dental assistants, and a front-office team.

How long has Hamilton Care Dental been serving Hamilton?

Since 2012. The practice has cared for thousands of Hamilton patients across more than a decade and has earned 4.6 stars from 100+ Google reviews.

Where is the dental clinic located?

Hamilton Care Dental Centre is at 969 Upper Ottawa St, 11 A, Hamilton, ON L8T 4V9, on Hamilton Mountain. Free parking is available on-site, and we are easy to reach from Stoney Creek, Ancaster, Dundas, and Binbrook.

What sets Hamilton Care Dental apart from other dentists?

Three dentists, a denturist, and registered dental hygienists in one office means most treatments happen here without a referral. We focus on conservative care, give written estimates before major work, bill insurance directly, accept CDCP, and offer Beautifi financing including 0% options.

Do you welcome new patients of all ages?

Yes. We see toddlers through seniors. First visits for kids focus on getting comfortable with the dental chair, and we always make time to answer questions for new adult patients.

What languages do your team speak?

English plus Arabic and other languages between team members. Let us know your preference when you book and we'll match you with a clinician you're comfortable talking to.
