What is the CDCP?

The Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP) is a federal program administered through Sun Life that helps eligible Canadians cover the cost of dental care. CDCP covers routine, restorative, and prosthetic dental services. Hamilton Care Dental Centre is proud to be an accepting provider.

Who is eligible?

To qualify for CDCP you must:

  • Be a Canadian resident for tax purposes
  • Not have access to private dental insurance
  • Have an adjusted family net income below the program threshold (eligibility expanded significantly in 2025 to include most adults)
  • Have filed your taxes the previous year

You can check eligibility and apply directly through the Government of Canada CDCP page. Once approved you'll receive a Sun Life member card with your CDCP coverage details.

What's covered?

CDCP covers a wide range of services, including:

  • Diagnostic services (exams, X-rays)
  • Preventive services (cleanings, fluoride, sealants)
  • Restorative services (fillings, crowns)
  • Endodontic services (root canals)
  • Periodontal services (gum care)
  • Prosthodontic services (dentures, partial dentures)
  • Oral surgery (extractions, wisdom teeth)

Some services require pre-authorization. We'll handle the submission and explain any potential co-pay before treatment begins.

How to use CDCP at our office

  1. Apply for CDCP through the Government of Canada portal
  2. Receive your Sun Life member ID
  3. Call us at (289) 755-2568 or book online
  4. Bring your CDCP card to your appointment
  5. We bill Sun Life directly, you only pay your co-pay if any

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept CDCP?

Yes. Hamilton Care Dental Centre accepts the Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP). We submit claims directly to Sun Life on your behalf.

Who qualifies for CDCP?

Canadian residents without private dental insurance whose adjusted family net income meets the program threshold. Eligibility expanded in 2025 to include most adults. Check your eligibility on the Government of Canada CDCP page.

What does CDCP cover?

CDCP covers a wide range of routine and restorative care: exams, cleanings, X-rays, fillings, root canal, extractions, dentures, and many other services. Some treatments may require pre-authorization.

Will I have a co-pay?

Depending on your income level, you may have a co-pay. We'll confirm this before treatment so there are no surprises.

How do I book a CDCP appointment?

Call (289) 755-2568 or book online. Have your CDCP member ID handy and we'll take care of the submission.
