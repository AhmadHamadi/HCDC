Our Story

Our Story

Hamilton Care Dental Centre opened on Upper Ottawa Street in 2012 with a small team and a single commitment: deliver thorough, gentle dentistry to every patient who walks through our door. A decade and tens of thousands of appointments later, that commitment hasn't changed, even as the practice has grown into a three-dentist team with registered dental hygienists, assistants, treatment coordinators, and a friendly front-office team.

What sets us apart is the time we take. Every treatment plan is explained fully. Every written estimate arrives before the work begins. And no one is rushed through a chair.

Our Exceptional Team

Three dentists with decades of combined experience. Registered dental hygienists who specialize in gentle, thorough preventive care. An in-house denturist, dental assistants, and a friendly front-office team that keeps every appointment running smoothly. We pick teammates who treat patients like family, then back them up with continuing education and modern equipment.

Continuous Innovation

Digital X-rays at a fraction of the radiation of older film. Intraoral scanners that replace messy impressions. 3D treatment planning for implants and Invisalign. Nitrous oxide sedation for anxious patients. We adopt new technology when it produces better outcomes for patients, not just because it's new.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our practice's history, team, and values.

When did Hamilton Care Dental Centre open?

Dr. Fadi Dawood opened the practice in 2012. More than a decade later, we serve thousands of patients across Hamilton Mountain, Stoney Creek, Ancaster, Dundas, and the surrounding region.

How big is the team today?

Three dentists (Dr. Dawood, Dr. Petros, Dr. Markho), a denturist (Saif), four registered dental hygienists (Darcy, Melissa, Maheen, Torees), three dental assistants, and a front-office team that includes the office manager and two dental receptionists.

What kind of technology do you use?

Digital X-rays at very low radiation, intraoral scanners that replace messy impressions, 3D treatment planning for implants and clear aligners, and nitrous oxide sedation for anxious patients. We adopt new tools when they produce better outcomes for patients.

What are your core values?

Excellence, Compassion, Integrity, and Community. We focus on delivering the highest quality of care, treating patients with kindness and respect, communicating honestly, and building lasting relationships with our Hamilton patients.

Why should I choose Hamilton Care Dental over other Hamilton clinics?

Three dentists plus a denturist and registered hygienists under one roof means most treatments happen here without referrals. We have a 4.6 star average from 100+ Google reviews, accept CDCP, offer Beautifi financing, provide written estimates, and never push elective treatment.
