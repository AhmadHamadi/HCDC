FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about visiting Hamilton Care Dental Centre? Here are answers to questions we hear most often.

Do you accept new patients?

Yes. Hamilton Care Dental Centre is always happy to welcome new patients. Whether you are looking for routine dental care, cosmetic treatments, or emergency services, our team is here to help you achieve and maintain a healthy smile.

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most major Canadian dental insurance plans, offer direct insurance billing, and accept CDCP (Canadian Dental Care Plan) for eligible patients. If you are unsure whether your plan is accepted, please call us at (289) 755-2568 and our friendly staff will verify your coverage.

Do you offer emergency dental services?

Yes. We offer same-day emergency dental care for severe tooth pain, broken or knocked-out teeth, infections, swelling, and lost fillings or crowns. Please call us as soon as possible so we can arrange prompt care.

What payment options are available?

We accept cash, debit, and all major credit cards. We also offer flexible payment plans and the Beautiful Loan dental financing program to make treatment more accessible for families.

How often should I visit the dentist?

Most patients should visit the dentist every six months for routine checkups and professional cleanings. Some patients with gum disease, orthodontic treatment, or other concerns may need more frequent visits. Our dentists will recommend a schedule that fits your needs.

Where is Hamilton Care Dental Centre located?

We are at 969 Upper Ottawa St, 11 A, Hamilton, ON L8T 4V9, conveniently serving Hamilton Mountain, Upper Ottawa, Stoney Creek, Ancaster, Dundas, and the surrounding Hamilton area.

Do you offer sedation for anxious patients?

Yes. We now offer nitrous oxide (laughing gas) sedation, a safe, gentle option that helps you stay calm and comfortable while remaining fully awake and responsive during your appointment.
